Curriculum Vitae

Short Bio

Before coming to Minnesota, I received my bachelor's degree in Applied Math the from Huazhong University of Science and Technology. Now, I am a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate in the Division of Biostatistics at the University of Minnesota. I am co-advised by Prof. Weihua Guan and Prof. Wei Pan and plan to graduate in the early summer of 2018.
