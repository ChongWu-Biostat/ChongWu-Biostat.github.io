Short Bio

Currently, I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Statistics at Florida State University. Prior to FSU, I was a biostatistics Ph.D. student at the University of Minnesota, co-advised by Prof. Weihua Guan and Prof. Wei Pan. I received my bachelor's degree in Applied Math the from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in 2013.
